Welcome to the ADD Bar Feedback Portal

If you've arrived here from the book, you already know what the ADD Bar is all about. If you're simply exploring the site—curious and wandering—let me shine a little light on this concept. The ADD Bar is a symbolic tool to reflect your decisions. Every choice we make—whether bold or uncertain—can be placed into one of three emotional boxes: Agree, Disagree, or Don’t Know.

But here's the twist: these boxes aren't just binary switches. Their depth is shaped by your emotional intensity—your feelings, your hesitations, your convictions. So while the categories may seem digital, the decisions themselves are deeply analog. They carry nuance, weight, and story.

Your feedback matters. Once you've completed all the chapters, you're invited to submit your reflections using the ADD Bar. Your input will help illuminate the emotional landscape of this book—and once enough responses are gathered, I’ll share the aggregated results with everyone. Thank you in advance for being part of this journey. Your voice adds dimension to the story.
