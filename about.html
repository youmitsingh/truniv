Because You Deserve More Than Just Information

Thank you for visiting this website and taking the time to explore the About section. If you’ve landed here, you’re likely seeking something more — clarity, insight, or perhaps a deeper understanding of life itself. That’s exactly what this book was written for.

Who am I?

My name is Youmit Singh. I’m an entrepreneur driven by ideas that aim to create meaningful impact — not for everyone, but for those who feel a connection to what I’ve built. My journey has taken me from childhood curiosity to college exploration, and eventually into the world of entrepreneurship. I’ve built products from scratch, launched them into the market, and even generated revenue from my very first startup in Mumbai.

Interestingly, reading and writing were never my strengths. But life has a way of surprising us — and sometimes, we find ourselves doing things we never imagined. This book is one of those surprises. It emerged from a series of personal and professional experiences, shaped by curiosity, reflection, and a desire to understand the world with empathy.

I’m a science and tech enthusiast at heart, always eager to learn and build solutions that address real societal needs. This book is an extension of that mindset — a research-driven exploration of life, not just a story.

What This Book Offers

This book is a journey into the deeper layers of human experience. It’s not about spiritual doctrines or philosophical abstractions — it’s about the why and what of life, more than the how. It explores how our lives are connected to universal truths, and how understanding those connections can bring clarity, purpose, and peace. Inside, you’ll discover:

  • Why we feel stuck in life — even when things seem successful on the surface
  • How decisions shape our path — into either a vicious cycle or a virtuous circle
  • The truth behind life and existence — and how to approach it with awareness
  • How the brain and mind coordinate — and influence our thoughts and actions
  • Why emotions matter — and how to navigate them effectively
  • The 10 Laws of Life — interconnected principles that guide personal growth
  • The role of energy and information — in shaping our internal and external worlds
  • Strategy beyond business — applying strategic thinking to personal goals
  • The true phases of life — and how to navigate them with intention
  • Removing procrastination and getting into productivity — Steps that makes things easier in your life.

More About My Background

I was raised in a small town in India, and later pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Technology from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. My career began in material science engineering, but over time, I shifted gears toward IT product development, marketing, and customer experience — turning ideas into tangible impact.

Everything you see on this website — from the design to the code — is crafted by me. I’m self-taught in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase cloud deployment. I wouldn’t call myself an expert, but I know enough to build prototypes and bring concepts to life. Beyond tech, I’m a socioeconomic enthusiast, always eager to understand how financial systems work — not just to grow, but to avoid the silent leakages that often go unnoticed.

Not long ago, I hit a wall in my professional life. Progress stalled, and I found myself questioning everything. That pause led to a powerful discovery: I was living with dyslexia and ADHD — a combination that had quietly shaped my communication and speech challenges for years. In a world that thrives on clarity and connection, this was no small hurdle. But now, I’m on a journey to understand it, work through it, and see how far I can go.

At the heart of the book is a recurring equation I’ve come to call “The Law of the Universe.” It’s a thread that runs through every chapter, offering a framework for understanding life beyond surface-level logic. This isn’t science fiction. It’s a fusion of science and spirituality — a genre I like to call a science novel. It’s grounded, insightful, and wrapped in the compelling story of three travelers whose journey mirrors the questions we all ask. If any part of this resonates with you, I’d love to hear your thoughts. You can reach me at youmit@tuniv.life.
